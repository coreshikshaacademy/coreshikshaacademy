About CORE SHIKSHA ACADEMY

Shaping Future Through Knowledge and Skills

Introduction

CORE SHIKSHA ACADEMY is committed to providing quality education combined with practical skills to empower students for future challenges. Established with the vision of bridging academic learning with professional development, our academy supports students from Grade 1 to 12 and offers specialized computer and professional courses. We strongly believe that education is not only about textbooks but about developing confidence, creativity, and technical know-how.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to deliver affordable and result-oriented education that prepares every learner to meet global standards. We aim to nurture curiosity, encourage innovation, and create leaders who can succeed in any field of life. The vision of CORE SHIKSHA ACADEMY is to become a leading educational institution where modern learning methods meet ethical values, ensuring students grow not only academically but also socially and emotionally.

Our Courses & Programs

At CORE SHIKSHA ACADEMY, we offer a wide range of programs to suit the needs of students. For school students, we provide subject coaching from Grade 1 to 12 in all major streams. For those aiming to build their career in technology and finance, our professional programs include Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA), Diploma in Financial Accounting (DFA), and industry-recognized Tally ERP training. We also conduct practical sessions in computer basics, web designing (HTML & CSS), and customized courses tailored to student interests and local demand.

Teaching Methodology

Our teaching methods are designed to ensure that learning is both enjoyable and effective. We focus on hands-on practice, project-based assignments, and personalized mentoring to keep students engaged. Small class sizes, interactive discussions, and regular assessments help students strengthen their knowledge and track their growth. By combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications, we ensure students are prepared to handle real-world situations confidently.

Faculty & Staff

CORE SHIKSHA ACADEMY is proud of its team of dedicated and experienced teachers. Our faculty includes academic experts who specialize in subjects across different streams, as well as practical trainers who focus on computer skills and professional courses. Beyond academics, our counseling and career guidance team ensures that students receive the right support to choose and excel in their desired career paths.
