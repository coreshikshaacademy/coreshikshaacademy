Class 1 — Foundation Builders

10-step vertical roadmap — step-by-step learning journey

1

Warm-up & Songs (5 min)

Action songs, rhymes & sensory activities to engage kids.

Short action-songs, fingerplays and a quick physical activity. Colorful props & sound cues for participation.

2

Alphabet Introduction (10–15 min)

Introduce 1–2 letters using phonics, flashcards & storytelling.

Teacher uses phonics songs, tracing, and matching games to build letter recognition and sound association.

3

Math Basics (10–15 min)

Counting with objects, number recognition and simple addition games.

Hands-on counting using beads, stones & flashcards. Simple oral addition using real objects helps concept formation.

4

Story Time (8–10 min)

Short stories with pictures and moral values.

Picture-based storytelling, question prompts and role-play to improve vocabulary & comprehension.

5

Activity Based Learning (12 min)

Drawing, clay modeling, matching cards to reinforce learning.

Creative activities linked to the lesson. Small group projects encourage cooperative learning and fine motor skills.

6

Hindi Basics (8–10 min)

Vowels, simple words and rhymes in Hindi.

Interactive rhymes, letter tracing and simple vocabulary exercises to strengthen bilingual skills.

7

General Awareness (6–8 min)

Topics: fruits, animals, family, seasons with charts.

Use real objects & charts. Quick Q&A to build observation and oral skills.

8

Worksheet Practice (10–12 min)

Writing, coloring and tracing worksheets for reinforcement.

Short, achievable tasks with teacher feedback. Worksheets reinforce classroom activity and develop pencil control.

9

Doubt Clearing (weekly)

Individual attention & small remedial groups.

Weekly remedial group and short 1:1 slots. Parents can send brief voice/video queries for teacher response.

10

Wrap-up & Home Task (5 min)

Recap, share home practice & appreciation.

Quick recap, show-and-tell, and a simple home practice activity. Positive reinforcement & sticker chart rewards.
